--- Short Description (132 chars max) ---
Automatically track price changes on your SaaS subscriptions. Get notified when Netflix, Spotify, or any subscription changes price.

--- Full Description ---
Never get surprised by a price hike again. PriceWatch automatically monitors your subscription services and notifies you when prices change.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Browse any subscription or pricing page
2. Click Track This in the PriceWatch popup to start monitoring
3. PriceWatch automatically checks for price changes every hour
4. Get instant browser notifications when prices go up or down

FEATURES:
• One-click auto-detection of prices on any subscription page
• Automatic hourly price checking
• Browser notifications when prices change
• Complete price history for every subscription
• Monthly spending overview
• Search and filter subscriptions
• Data export/backup
• Optional cloud sync across devices (Pro feature coming soon)

USE CASES:
• Track SaaS subscription prices (Netflix, Spotify, AWS, GitHub, etc.)
• Monitor hosting plan prices
• Watch for price drops on services you're considering
• Keep an eye on competitor pricing

PRICING:
• Free: Track up to 3 subscriptions
• Pro (/mo): Track up to 50 subscriptions with email alerts (coming soon)

WHAT MAKES PRICEWATCH DIFFERENT:
• Works automatically - just browse normally
• No manual entry needed for most subscriptions
• Price detection engine handles multiple currencies and formats
• Full privacy: data stays on your device (cloud sync is optional)
